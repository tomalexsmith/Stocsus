The problem >

The global parts shortage is creating significant challenges for Tharsus as a manufacturer. Frequently having to manually search websites to check for stock and pricing on components has become an issue.

Our solution >

Our aim was to develop a stock and price checker application that allows workers at Tharsus to automatically and rapidly search a range of websites for a list of parts, eliminating the current long and tedious process.

How to get started

All the steps you need to get started using Stocsus - fast searches for
thousands of parts.

Step 1

Sign up with and email and password.

Or alternatively login to an existing account.

Step 2

Search individual parts by part no., quantity and no. of models.

Or search multiple parts at a time by uploading an excel spreadsheet.

Step 3

View of suppliers which have the required quantity for each searched part.

Or save results for later by downloading them as a .cvs file.

About us
Developed for the team at Tharsus A project by team 18

The problem

The global parts shortage is creating significant challenges for Tharsus as a manufacturer. Frequently having to manually search websites to check for stock and pricing on components has become an issue.

Our solution

Our aim was to develop a stock and price checking application that allows workers at Tharsus to automatically and rapidly search a range of websites for a list of parts, eliminating the current long and tedious process.

Step 1

Step 2

Step 3
